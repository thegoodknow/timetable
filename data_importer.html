Timetable Data Importer (HTML to JSON)

Steps to Update Timetable:

  1. UPLOAD: Click "Choose File" below and upload your existing **`timetable_data.json`**.
  2. PASTE: Paste the raw HTML of the **new week's** timetable into the text area.
  3. CONVERT: Click "Convert & Download." The tool will **merge** the new week into your existing data and prompt you to download the updated file.
  4. SAVE & UPLOAD: Save the downloaded file as **`timetable_data.json`** and **overwrite** the file in your GitHub repository.
  5. RETURN: Click "Go Back to Timetable" to view the changes.
